Project description

Project description for a 5 year old (which problem do you solve, for who do you solve it (client + oSoc) )

For Selor an agency that provides recruiting services for over 150 different governmental institutes, we build an application to ease the scoring of candidates in face-2-face interviews.

With Revit, the judges at Selor are able to quote any job candidate from any device, whether it's a tablet, a smartphone or a laptop. A huge step up from pencils and paper for both the judges, the candidates and the administrative services at Selor.

Revit is build using open-source components and open development to ensure easy adaptability in the future. The application was designed, built, evaluated and deployed during an intensive 4 week program at open Summer of code, an initiative that allows students to work on real world applications for companies and governmental institutes.
